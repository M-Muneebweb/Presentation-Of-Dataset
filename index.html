üìã Dataset Columns - Basic Info

Song Identification

  • track_id: Unique song identifier
  • track_name: Name of the song
  • artist_name: Who performed it
  • genre: Music category (pop, rock, etc.)

Basic Properties

  • popularity: How popular the song is (0-100)
  • duration_ms: Song length in milliseconds
  • key: Musical key of the song
  • mode: Major (1) or Minor (0) scale

üéµ Dataset Columns - Audio Features

Energy & Mood

  • energy: How energetic the song feels
  • danceability: How suitable for dancing
  • valence: Musical positivity (happy/sad)
  • tempo: Speed of the song (BPM)

Technical Aspects

  • acousticness: How acoustic vs electronic
  • instrumentalness: Amount of vocals vs instruments
  • liveness: Recorded live or in studio
  • speechiness: Amount of spoken words
